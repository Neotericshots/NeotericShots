About

Neoteric Shots is a photography name created by myself, Aaron Waychoff, A 20 year old student attenting the University of Cincinnati for Electronic Media Tech & Communications. I'm currently focused on Photography, Video & Film, Animation, and Graphic Design. I started photography in 2014 before getting my very own, more fancy camera to shoot with. Eventually I started taking photos for close family and friends. With several years of training, practicing, studying, and dedication, I've begun exploring new areas of photography
